05 - 06 Temmuz 2006 > Rapid Manufacturing - First International Conference:
Ülke / Þehir: Ýngiltere / Nottingham
Yer: Loughborough University

The First International conference on Rapid Manufacturing will include a mixture of adacemic and industrial presentations focused on both RM research and applications.

Conference focus:

The emergence of Rapid Manufacturing is truly leading to a "new industrial revolution for the digital age" and will enable a step-change for manufacturers that will ultimately result in manufacturing being maintained in high-wage economies. The advantages that RM brings are already being commercially exploited by organisations such as Airbus, Boeing, Siemens Hearing Solutions, Bentley and many others to gain a commercial advantage today. Much academic research is also being undertaken and it is the aim of this conference to impart to industry and academia the state-of-the-art in Rapid Manufacturing so that commercial advantage can be exploited and new avenues can be explored.
