 About Us:

MfgTR is an online marketplace that enables manufacturing buyers and engineers to find Turkish machine shops and suppliers of engineered, made-to-order components.

For your current and future outsourcing needs, if you are looking for high quality manufacturing services at low cost, you are in the right place; MfgTR is established to match you with qualified Turkish companies that use latest CAD/CAM/CAE, RE, RP and CNC technologies.

The RFQ posting is very easy, fast and effective. Our business is to match you with the proper Turkish companies that have the correct capabilities and experience for your needs...
