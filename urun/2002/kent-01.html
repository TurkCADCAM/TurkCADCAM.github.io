For Release Wednesday, July 3, 2002

Ricoh Expands use of Solid Edge Software from EDS Leading printer and copier manufacturer cites ease-of-use and increased 3D design productivity; expects accelerated design and increased product quality


PLANO, Texas - EDS announced today that Ricoh Company Ltd., one of the world's leading suppliers of office automation equipment, has expanded its use of Solid Edge® software for the computer-aided design (CAD) of printers and copiers at its manufacturing facilities in Japan and other global locations.

Ricoh selected Solid Edge based on its ease of use and increased 3D capabilities. With its recent expansion, Ricoh is now using over 100 Solid Edge licenses.

"We felt Solid Edge would help us move more quickly to 3D technology than any other system available," said Satoh Toshiaki, Manager, Ricoh Development Process Innovation Department. "We have successfully trained more than 250 engineers at Ricoh to work with Solid Edge."

Solid Edge is being used for the design and manufacturing of Ricoh's latest line of market-leading printers and copiers, including state-of-the-art multifunctional products that provide print, fax and scanning functionality.

Ricoh engineers also have applauded new CAD innovations following their hands-on testing last month of Solid Edge Version 12. "We originally selected Solid Edge because of its usability," said Ricoh Research and Development Engineer Etsuro Kuretani. "Version 12 again validates that choice by providing an easy-to-use 3D wiring design capability for our printers and copiers. Currently, we must add wiring into the 2D drawing after the product is mostly designed. Now we can save significant time by including wiring and checking for interferences with other parts as a natural step throughout the 3D design process."

Solid Edge (www.solidedge.com) from EDS is a powerful 3D CAD software that can reduce manufacturing companies' time to market by 50 percent. The exclusive Solid Edge Design with Insight knowledge-sharing technology embeds design process management capabilities directly within CAD to provide better quality products, lower costs and shorter delivery cycles. Solid Edge designers at thousands of companies worldwide, including Alcoa, NEC Engineering and Volvo, easily and quickly create, capture and share product knowledge across the entire engineering organization. The Solid Edge Voyager Program includes more than 150 integrated engineering software applications and computer hardware solutions.

About Ricoh

Ricoh Company Ltd. is one of the world's leading suppliers of office automation equipment, including copiers, facsimile machines, data processing systems, optical disk drives and related supplies. The Company is also renowned for its state-of-the-art electronic devices and photographic equipment. Ricoh is consolidating its traditional areas of strength through digital and color technologies to build the image processing equipment and systems that are increasingly crucial to the creative needs of today's networked offices. To remain at the forefront of change, Ricoh maintains a fully integrated research and development structure that has accelerated the introduction of innovative, competitive products to the marketplace.
